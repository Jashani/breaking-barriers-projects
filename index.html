About the Hackathon

Breaking Barriers to AI Safety was a hackathon at the London Initiative for Safe AI (LISA), built for generalists and individuals from less-technical fields to get hands-on AI safety experience. Teams chose from suggested projects or came up with their own, alongside a bingo track of smaller tasks to help people explore their interests and build mini proof-of-work artifacts.

We had over 75 participants and 28 submitted projects. The room was full of talented people, but the depth, breadth, and quality of the work still went far beyond what we could have imagined. They showed us that you don't need a traditional background to do useful, ambitious work in AI safety.

Hosted by LISA × BlueDot Impact.
